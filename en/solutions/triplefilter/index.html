Is it true?

🤔

Have you made absolutely sure that what you are about to tell me is true?.

🤔

Is it good?

Is what you are about to tell me something good and kind?

Is it useful?

🤔

Is what you are about to tell me going to be useful for me to know?

If it's not:

  • True
  • Good
  • Useful
